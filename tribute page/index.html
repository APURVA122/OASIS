Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About Him

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India’s civilian space program and military missile development, earning him the title “Missile Man of India.”

Achievements

  • Developed India’s first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests (1998)
  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Received Bharat Ratna in 1997

Famous Quote

“Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep.”
